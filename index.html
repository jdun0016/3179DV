Which Country has the best Netflix deal?


Netflix is one of the biggest streaming services around the world and is enjoyed in many countries. However, depending on where you live in the world, different countries may have different subscription prices and different TV and movie options. Lets find out where around the world the best Netflix deal can be found.



Netflix Standard Monthly Costs

This map displays the standard netflix monthly subscription cost around the world in USD. The cost of a standard subscription is most expensive around Europe. It is cheapest in India, Turkey and many places around South America including Brazil, Argentina and Colombia.




Netflix Biggest Libraries

A Netflix Library is made up of TV shows and Movies available on Netflix at a certain time in a particular country. Czechia has the biggest library with 7,325 available programs to view. Slovakia is the only other country to have over 7000 items in their library with 7,035. Australia rounds out the top 15 countries, having 6,114 movies and TV shows. However, not all of these programs in each country are available with standard subscriptions. To access all the programs, a premium Netflix subscription is required. Lets see how much it would cost to acquire a premium subscription, and how this compares to other countries.

Premium Subscription Value

A premium monthly subscription to Netflix can be quite expensive in some countries as it can cost over $25 USD. Good value for money can be found in Turkey who match up reasonably well with other countries in terms of range of program options and have a very good price of $4.02. India has even greater range at quite a low price of $8.60, but this is still double the cost of Turkey's premium fee. San Marino, Croatia and Liechtenstein have the worst premium deals as they have much smaller libraries than the other countries and still charge a costly monthly price of $20.32, $13.54 and $26.96 respectively.




Price Change for Subscription Level

Taking a look at countries with the top 15 library sizes, lets see how the level of subscription price varies. The pricing is quite similar for a basic subscription with the cheapest belong to the Philippines at $7.35 and the most expensive being Singapore at $9.51. There is a clear best and worst country from the graphic for the standard and premium prices. Gibraltar costs $14.67 and $20.32 for a standard and premium subscription respectively, whereas the Philippines cost $9.14 and $10.93.

Thus, it is clear that the Phillipines have the best Netflix deal accross all categories with large libraries and reasonable and affordable prices for all forms of subscription.
